About Me

The eyes of mine see the world with quiet detachment, observing its patterns, shadows, and subtle truths. Emotions, though distant, become colors and shapes on my canvas. Art lets me speak what I do not feel aloud — a mirror to my inner stillness and unspoken reflections.

Exploring creativity through skill & soul. Always wondered how art feels from every otehr perspective and then realised there is just a feel that we experience in an art!

Drifting in a fragile paper boat, the soul sails vast uncertainties, proving even delicate dreams carry courage strong enough to cross oceans.

With only a brush in fragile wings, art lifts the heaviest emotions, carrying silent storms effortlessly, feel weightless in expression.

Drawn beyond the canvas, the soul longs for art’s embrace, where painted arms radiate warmth, belonging, and a quiet promise of connection.
